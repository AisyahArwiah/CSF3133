About UMT

Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) is a public university located in Kuala Nerus, Terengganu, Malaysia. The university focuses on marine science, oceanography, environmental studies, food science, and maritime technology.

UMT began as Universiti Putra Malaysia Terengganu (UPMT) before evolving into Kolej Universiti Sains dan Teknologi Malaysia (KUSTEM) and finally receiving full university status as Universiti Malaysia Terengganu in 2007. Today, UMT continues to lead in marine and aquatic research in Malaysia and Southeast Asia.
